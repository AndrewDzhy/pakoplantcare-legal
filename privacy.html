Privacy Policy for Pako Plant Care

Effective date: March 28, 2026
Last updated: March 28, 2026

Pako Plant Care (“we”, “us”, “our”) provides plant identification, plant health check, and care reminder features in the mobile app Pako Plant Care (the “App”). This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Data We Collect

  • Photos and scan input: plant photos you capture/select for identification and health checks.
  • Plant and care data: plant profiles, care preferences, reminders, diagnosis history, care logs.
  • Subscription data: purchase/restore/entitlement status via Apple + RevenueCat.
  • Analytics and diagnostics: app events and technical performance metadata.
  • Security data: app/service identifiers and App Attest-related security tokens/assertions.

2. How We Use Data

  • Provide scan and health-check results.
  • Save plants, logs, and reminders.
  • Manage subscriptions and restore purchases.
  • Improve reliability, performance, and quality.
  • Prevent abuse and protect service security.

3. Where Data Is Stored

  • Primary app content is stored locally on your device.
  • If Apple backup is enabled on your device, local app data may be included in backups.
  • No account-based cloud sync unless explicitly stated in-app.

4. Third-Party Processors

  • Apple (StoreKit / App Store)
  • RevenueCat
  • Cloudflare (proxy infrastructure)
  • Plant.id
  • PostHog

5. Permissions

  • Camera: capture plant photos for scan/diagnosis.
  • Photo Library: import photos; add/save only when you choose.
  • Notifications: plant care reminders.

6. Data Retention

  • Local data stays until deletion/uninstall.
  • Temporary cache can be cleared in Settings.
  • Server-side logs/analytics are retained for limited operational periods.

7. Your Choices

  • Revoke permissions in iOS Settings.
  • Delete plants and related records in-app.
  • Clear temporary cache in Settings.
  • Uninstall app to remove local app data from device.

8. Children

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or minimum local age).

9. International Processing

Providers may process data outside your country with appropriate safeguards.

10. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards, including secure transport and request authentication.

11. Changes

We may update this policy and will revise the “Last updated” date.
